🏺 兵马俑是什么？

1974年，几个农民打井时意外发现了地下的陶俑碎片。考古学家赶来一挖——挖出了8000多个真人大小的陶兵陶马，震惊全世界，被称为"世界第八大奇迹"。
In 1974, farmers digging a well stumbled upon clay fragments. Archaeologists uncovered over 8,000 life-sized clay soldiers and horses — the "Eighth Wonder of the World."

这是2200多年前秦始皇给自己造的"地下军队"。秦始皇是中国第一个皇帝，统一了中国，他相信死后还需要一支军队保护。所以他命人用陶土烧制了一整支军队——步兵、骑兵、弓箭手、将军、战车、战马——每一个士兵的脸都不一样！
This is the "underground army" of Qin Shi Huang, China's first emperor, made over 2,200 years ago. He unified China and believed he'd need an army in the afterlife. So he ordered a full army made from clay — infantry, cavalry, archers, generals, chariots, horses — and every single soldier has a unique face!

一号坑最大，6000多个士兵排成方阵。二号坑有特殊兵种和正在挖掘的考古现场。三号坑最小，是"指挥部"。
Pit 1 is the biggest — 6,000+ soldiers in formation. Pit 2 has special troops and an active dig site. Pit 3 is the smallest — it's the "command center."

找找看：跪射俑的手掌上有掌纹！鞋底有针眼纹路！还有一个神秘的绿脸俑——没人知道为什么他的脸是绿色的。
Look for: The kneeling archer has palm lines on his hands! His shoe soles have tiny stitch marks! And there's a mysterious green-faced warrior — nobody knows why his face is green.
